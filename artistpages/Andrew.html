Andrew vanHaaster - La Criatura

Howdy y’all, my name is Andrew vanHaaster (He/Him) and I am an aspiring 3D artist with an emphasis on organic modeling, specifically for creatures! I love all things animal and badass, and this has shown from a very young age. Growing up, my favourite movies were movies any other child would’ve probably been frightened of: Jurassic Park and Godzilla. If it isn’t obvious just from that alone, I grew up being a peculiar child, taking interests in very niche things and fascinated with the nature of the world. While other kids played tag and hide and seek, I was more caught up in building worlds and infrastructures for local wild life, specifically when I had watched ants battle each other: “Quick! Make way for the throne, the queen has been defeated” said ant number 1. This habit carried me throughout high school, where I had grown my love for storytelling and drawing, and eventually into college, where I was finally brave enough to take a risk and pursue my craft, in hopes that one day I may share my crazy wacky stories to the world! INTO THE TRAIL OF SHRINES! 🐉⛩️
