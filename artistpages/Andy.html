Andy Kim - The Soldier

I am Andy Kim, an artist focusing on storyboarding and animation, but well-versed in other aspects of art. I personally interested in the process of world-building and connecting it to the stories and characters. It feels like solving a complex puzzle making sure everything in the story aligns with the world it is taking place in. Hopefully in the future I am able to work in this field and continue to love the creative process alongside with my peers.
