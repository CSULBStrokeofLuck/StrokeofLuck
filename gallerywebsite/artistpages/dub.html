dub Nelson - El Barril

I’m dub. I animate and write stories about little creatures in absurd situations. I like animals, inanimate objects, and drinking milk. I strongly dislike prison. My work is characterized by thick lines, crosshatching, and the use of mixed mediums (mainly the combination of 2d and CG animation). Ever since George Bush’s presidency, I’ve been trying to reclaim the part of me that was lost in it. I have a fascination for post-9/11 art, and seek to recapture the essence contained within art pieces from that era. Thanks!
