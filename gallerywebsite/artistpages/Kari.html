Kari Teranishi - The Dragon

Hi! My name is Kari Teranishi and I'm a pre-production major! I have a passion for creation, which strongly influenced my interest in character design, visual development, and storytelling! I take inspiration for my work from many of my favorite games and strive to reach others in the same way these stories have reached me! Aside from art I always have loved animals of all kinds and have dabbled in the hobby of keeping reptiles and inverts! I also recently have been invested in collecting plants of all kinds! I hope to always invest passion and love into anything I end up pursuing in the future, and I enjoy sharing these things with others no matter what it is! I appreciate the time taken to take a look at my work, and I hope to continue bringing joy and creativity into the world!
