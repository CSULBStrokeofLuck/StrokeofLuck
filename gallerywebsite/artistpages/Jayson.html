Jayson Covarrubias - La Rana

Hello hello!! My name is Jayson Covarrubias! I’m a 2D character designer especially interested in creature design. I love the process of telling stories through a character’s design and I really strive to incorporate that into my work as much as I can! I get a lot of my inspiration from video games that I grew up with and would love to work in that field in the (hopefully) near future! I’m both nervous and excited about post-college life and can’t wait to see where my peers and I will be in the art world!!!
