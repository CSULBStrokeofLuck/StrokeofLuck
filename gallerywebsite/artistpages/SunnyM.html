Sunny Morgan - La Araña

Hi hello hello hi my name is Sunny, I’m a Pre-Production major mostly focused on character design and vis dev but more recently have been craving to move away from digital art and rekindle my love for traditional illustration. This summer I’m thrilled to be taking my last class with CSULB abroad in Dublin, Ireland where we’ll be learning plein air painting with gouache 🍀

I am always amazed and inspired by nature as well as all kinds of animals, bugs, creatures, critters, and creepy crawlers, but especially cats 🪲🐈‍⬛💜

Post graduation, I want to use my newfound free time to explore other physical mediums like clay/ceramics, printmaking, tattooing, and whatever else comes my way! I also play guitar and am very excited to start making music with friends :D

No matter the medium or the goal I hope to never stop creating and connecting with people through art
