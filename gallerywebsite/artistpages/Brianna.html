Brianna Barrera - The Fool

Hi I’m Brianna Barrera I’m a preproduction major who loves character exploration and environmental design. My work is mostly experimental storytelling inspired, with that in-between feeling of something a little unsettling and surreal but grounding. I’ve been into video games and animation since I was a kid, especially the behind-the-scenes side of it. The creativity , passion and dedication of turning an idea in someone’s head into a 2d story made me curious and excited to express. I truly believe art resonates differently with everyone, and I love thinking about all the different ways a piece can be experienced. I'm genuinely so happy I've been able to experience so much here.
